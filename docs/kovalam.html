Kovalam - Beach Paradise

Relax and rejuvenate in the sun-kissed beaches of Kovalam, Kerala's famous coastal retreat.

About Kovalam

Kovalam, located in Kerala, is renowned for its stunning beaches, clear waters, and palm-lined coast. This popular beach destination has three main beaches: Lighthouse Beach, Hawah Beach, and Samudra Beach, each offering unique experiences.

The town is a major tourist attraction due to its tranquility, perfect for sunbathing, swimming, and water sports. Kovalam is also known for its Ayurvedic resorts, making it a wellness destination in addition to its natural beauty.

Top 4 Tourist Spots in Kovalam

  • Lighthouse Beach

    The most famous beach in Kovalam, known for its iconic lighthouse. The beach offers stunning views of the Arabian Sea and is ideal for swimming and beach activities.

  • Hawah Beach

    A quieter beach, perfect for those looking for a peaceful retreat. It's also a great spot for yoga and meditation by the sea.

  • Samudra Beach

    Less crowded and more secluded, Samudra Beach is perfect for travelers seeking a peaceful escape in nature, with opportunities for beach walks and photography.

  • Kovalam Beach Viewpoint

    For a breathtaking view of the Kovalam coastline, visit the viewpoint located on the hill. It offers panoramic views of the town and surrounding beaches.

What Food Items to Try

  • Kerala Sadya: A traditional vegetarian feast served on a banana leaf, comprising rice, curries, chutneys, and desserts like payasam.
  • Fish Moilee: A flavorful fish curry cooked in coconut milk, commonly found along the coastal regions of Kerala.
  • Puttu and Kadala Curry: A popular Kerala breakfast dish consisting of steamed rice cakes (puttu) served with a spicy black chickpea curry (kadala).
  • Kerala Parotta: A flaky, layered flatbread usually served with curries, especially meat or vegetable dishes.
  • Banana Fritters: A delicious snack made from ripe bananas, deep-fried and served with a sprinkling of sugar or honey.
