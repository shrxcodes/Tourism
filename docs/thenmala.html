About Thenmala

Thenmala is not just a haven for eco-tourism but also a place where you can explore various outdoor activities, including trekking, boating, and bird watching. The destination also offers a great blend of cultural experiences, with local tribal villages and their traditions adding to the charm.

Top 4 Tourist Spots in Thenmala

  • Thenmala Dam

    The dam offers stunning views and is a great place for a peaceful getaway, surrounded by forest and hills.

  • Shenduruny Wildlife Sanctuary

    A rich sanctuary known for its biodiversity, where visitors can spot a variety of wildlife and birds.

  • Adventure Zone

    Thenmala is famous for its adventure activities, including zip-lining, mountain biking, and boating in the picturesque surroundings.

  • Butterfly Park

    A dedicated park for nature enthusiasts to learn about the various species of butterflies native to the region.

What Food Items to Try

  • Kerala Sadya: A traditional Kerala feast served on a banana leaf, consisting of various vegetarian dishes, rice, and dessert.
  • Puttu and Kadala Curry: A traditional Kerala breakfast of steamed rice cakes served with spicy black chickpea curry.
  • Karimeen Pollichathu: A famous Kerala dish made with pearl spot fish, marinated with spices, and grilled in banana leaves.
  • Appam and Stew: A combination of soft rice pancakes served with a delicious coconut-based stew, often with vegetables or chicken.
  • Vishu Kanji: A traditional Kerala dish made from rice and coconut milk, typically enjoyed during the Vishu festival.
