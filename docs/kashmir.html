About Jammu & Kashmir

Jammu and Kashmir, often called "Paradise on Earth," is located in the northernmost region of India. Known for its majestic mountains, picturesque valleys, and crystal-clear lakes, the region is a haven for nature lovers and spiritual seekers alike.

Whether it's the snow-capped peaks of Gulmarg, the enchanting Dal Lake in Srinagar, or the religious significance of Vaishno Devi in Jammu, the state offers a diverse array of attractions. It's also known for its rich crafts, Pashmina shawls, dry fruits, and the warm hospitality of its people.

What Food Items to Try

  • Rogan Josh: A flavorful lamb curry cooked with aromatic spices and yogurt.
  • Kashmiri Pulao: Saffron-infused rice dish garnished with dry fruits and a hint of sweetness.
  • Dum Aloo: Baby potatoes cooked in a rich yogurt-based gravy with Kashmiri spices.
  • Gushtaba: Minced meatballs in a creamy yogurt gravy, traditionally served at royal feasts.
  • Noon Chai: A salty pink tea flavored with baking soda and milk, usually enjoyed with bread.
