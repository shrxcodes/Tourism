Jaisalmer - The Golden City

Explore the majestic desert city of Jaisalmer, rich in history, culture, and golden sandstone architecture.

About Jaisalmer

Jaisalmer, located in the heart of the Thar Desert in Rajasthan, is famously known as the Golden City due to its yellow sandstone architecture. The city is crowned by the stunning Jaisalmer Fort, which rises from the desert like a mirage.

With a rich blend of Rajputana heritage, desert charm, and intricate architecture, Jaisalmer offers an unforgettable experience. Its vibrant bazaars, traditional music, and desert safaris attract travelers from across the globe.

Top 4 Tourist Spots in Jaisalmer

  • Jaisalmer Fort

    Also known as Sonar Quila, this massive fort houses palaces, temples, and shops, offering panoramic views of the city and desert.

  • Sam Sand Dunes

    Located on the outskirts, this is the perfect spot for desert safaris, camel rides, and enjoying traditional Rajasthani performances under the stars.

  • Patwon Ki Haveli

    A group of five intricately carved havelis showcasing beautiful jharokhas, mirror work, and paintings, reflecting the affluent lifestyle of merchants.

  • Gadisar Lake

    An artificial lake surrounded by temples and ghats, offering peaceful boat rides and scenic sunrise/sunset views.
