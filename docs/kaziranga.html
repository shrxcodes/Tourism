Kaziranga National Park

Explore the wilderness and diverse wildlife of Kaziranga, home to the famous Indian One-Horned Rhinoceros.

About Kaziranga

Kaziranga National Park, located in Assam, India, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is famous for being home to the endangered Indian One-Horned Rhinoceros. Spanning over 1,000 square miles, the park is a haven for wildlife enthusiasts, featuring vast grasslands, wetlands, and dense forests. In addition to rhinos, it also houses tigers, elephants, and a variety of bird species, making it a top destination for wildlife safaris.

Founded in 1905, the park’s landscape offers an extraordinary blend of rivers, forests, and rolling hills, and it is a vital part of India’s conservation efforts. Kaziranga has earned a reputation for successfully increasing the population of rhinos and other endangered species through conservation efforts and active management.

Wildlife to Spot

  • Indian One-Horned Rhinoceros

    The star attraction of Kaziranga, the Indian One-Horned Rhinoceros is a majestic creature that roams freely across the park.

  • Royal Bengal Tiger

    Though elusive, the Royal Bengal Tiger can be spotted in the park, making for an unforgettable wildlife experience.

  • Asian Elephant

    Known for their enormous size and gentle nature, Asian elephants are a common sight in the park, particularly in the western range.

  • Swamp Deer (Barasingha)

    Native to the area, the Swamp Deer is often seen in the park's marshy areas and is a vulnerable species.

  • Birds

    The park is a paradise for bird watchers, with over 500 species of birds, including pelicans, storks, and kingfishers. Migratory birds are especially abundant during the winter months.

Safari Experiences

  • Jeep Safari

    The most popular safari, allowing tourists to explore the park's diverse terrains and spot large animals like rhinos and tigers.

  • Elephant Safari

    Get a closer view of wildlife, particularly rhinos, from the back of an elephant. These safaris typically take place in the central zone.

  • Bird Watching Tours

    Ideal for nature lovers, these tours focus on spotting the wide variety of birds that inhabit or migrate to the park.

  • Photography Tours

    Specialized safaris for photographers, providing ample opportunities to capture the beauty of Kaziranga's wildlife and landscapes.
