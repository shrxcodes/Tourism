Explore the pristine beauty, exotic beaches, and rich marine life of the Andaman and Nicobar Islands.

About Andaman and Nicobar

The Andaman and Nicobar Islands, located in the Bay of Bengal, are a group of stunning islands known for their pristine beaches, lush forests, and diverse marine life. The islands are a perfect destination for nature lovers, offering a peaceful escape with an array of water sports, wildlife, and untouched natural beauty.

With tropical weather, crystal-clear waters, and an incredible range of flora and fauna, the Andaman and Nicobar Islands are an ideal destination for tourists seeking adventure, relaxation, or a mix of both. Popular spots include Havelock Island, Radhanagar Beach, Cellular Jail, and more.

Top 4 Tourist Spots in Andaman and Nicobar

  • Radhanagar Beach

    Radhanagar Beach on Havelock Island is renowned for its stunning beauty, with turquoise waters, white sandy shores, and amazing sunset views.

  • Cellular Jail

    A colonial-era prison, Cellular Jail is a historical monument that stands as a reminder of India’s struggle for independence. Visitors can explore its dark history through a light and sound show.

  • Neil Island

    Neil Island is a small, tranquil island with beautiful beaches, coral reefs, and water sports activities. It’s perfect for those seeking solitude and natural beauty.

  • Elephant Beach

    Elephant Beach, located in Havelock, is famous for water sports, snorkeling, and its vibrant coral reefs. It’s a great spot for those looking to explore marine life up close.

What Food Items to Try

  • Seafood: The islands offer fresh, delicious seafood such as prawns, fish, crabs, and lobster, often cooked with local spices.
  • Coconut-based Dishes: Dishes made with coconut milk and grated coconut are popular in Andaman cuisine, including seafood curries.
  • Fish Curry Rice: A traditional dish made with rice and fish curry, this is a staple food in Andaman.
  • Local Sweets: The islands also have a variety of traditional sweets made from coconut and jaggery, like 'Khopra Pak' and 'Coconut Barfi'.
  • Vegetarian Delights: For vegetarians, Andaman offers dishes like dal, vegetable curry, and rice made with locally grown vegetables.
