Alleppey - Venice of the East

Explore the serene backwaters, houseboats, and lush landscapes of Alleppey in Kerala.

About Alleppey

Alleppey, also known as Alappuzha, is a charming town located in Kerala, India, famous for its extensive network of canals, backwaters, and houseboats. Often referred to as the "Venice of the East," Alleppey offers a unique experience with its tranquil boat rides through lush landscapes and traditional Kerala village life.

The town is also home to beautiful beaches, temples, and lush paddy fields, making it a peaceful escape for nature lovers and travelers seeking an authentic experience of Kerala’s rural beauty.

Top 4 Tourist Spots in Alleppey

  • Alleppey Backwaters

    The Alleppey Backwaters are the town’s most famous attraction, offering serene boat rides through narrow canals surrounded by lush green vegetation and traditional Kerala village life.

  • Alleppey Beach

    Alleppey Beach offers a peaceful retreat with golden sands, scenic views, and a beautiful lighthouse. It's perfect for a relaxing stroll or enjoying a sunset.

  • Vembanad Lake

    Vembanad Lake is the largest lake in Kerala, offering picturesque views, boat rides, and bird-watching opportunities, especially in the winter months.

  • Krishnapuram Palace

    A beautiful 18th-century palace, known for its Kerala-style architecture and the famous mural painting of Gajendra Moksha, depicting the story of an elephant’s salvation.

What Food Items to Try

  • Karimeen Pollichathu: A traditional Kerala dish made with pearl spot fish, marinated in a blend of spices and wrapped in banana leaves, then grilled.
  • Appam and Stew: Soft rice pancakes served with a flavorful coconut milk-based vegetable stew.
  • Puttu and Kadala Curry: Steamed rice cakes served with a spicy black chickpea curry, a popular breakfast dish in Kerala.
  • Fish Curry: A tangy and spicy curry made with fresh fish and local Kerala spices, often served with rice or appam.
  • Banana Chips: Crispy, fried banana chips made from raw bananas, often enjoyed as a snack or with meals.
