About Bandipur

Bandipur National Park, located in Karnataka, India, is one of the most well-known wildlife sanctuaries in southern India. Situated at the foothills of the Western Ghats, Bandipur is a haven for diverse wildlife and offers stunning landscapes of dense forests, rivers, and grasslands. Established in 1974, the park was initially a hunting reserve for the Maharaja of Mysore and later transformed into a protected area as part of Project Tiger.

The park covers an area of 874 square kilometers and is part of the Nilgiri Biosphere Reserve, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Bandipur is famous for its tiger population and is home to a wide range of animals including elephants, leopards, Indian bison, and a variety of bird species.

Wildlife to Spot

  • Bengal Tiger

    Bandipur is known for its tiger sightings, making it one of the best places in India to spot the majestic Bengal Tiger in its natural habitat.

  • Indian Elephant

    The park is home to large herds of Indian elephants, which can often be seen grazing or roaming in the open grasslands.

  • Leopard

    Leopards are another big draw for wildlife enthusiasts in Bandipur, though their elusive nature makes them harder to spot than tigers.

  • Indian Bison (Gaur)

    Indian Bison, also known as Gaur, are the largest species of wild cattle and can be found in large groups in Bandipur.

  • Birds

    With over 200 species of birds, Bandipur is a paradise for bird watchers. Species like the Malabar Whistling Thrush, the Grey-headed Bulbul, and many migratory birds make the park their home.

Safari Experiences

  • Jeep Safari

    The jeep safari is the most popular way to explore Bandipur National Park, allowing visitors to spot tigers, elephants, and other wildlife in their natural habitat.

  • Bus Safari

    For larger groups, the bus safari offers a great way to explore the park with the added advantage of covering larger areas and increasing the chances of spotting wildlife.

  • Bird Watching Tours

    Bandipur's diverse bird species make it an ideal spot for bird watching, with dedicated tours for nature enthusiasts and bird photographers.
