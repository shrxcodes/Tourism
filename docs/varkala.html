Varkala - The Cliffside Beach

Relax and unwind in Varkala, a stunning coastal town known for its cliffs, beaches, and vibrant atmosphere.

About Varkala

Varkala, located in Kerala, is a beautiful beach destination famous for its striking cliffs that rise above the Arabian Sea. The town is renowned for its Papanasam Beach, where locals believe that a dip in the water cleanses the soul. Varkala has a spiritual charm, with the ancient Janardhana Swamy Temple located nearby.

Top 4 Tourist Spots in Varkala

  • Papanasam Beach

    The most famous beach in Varkala, believed to have spiritual significance. A popular spot for swimming and sunbathing, with stunning cliff views.

  • Janardhana Swamy Temple

    An ancient Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu, located near Papanasam Beach. The temple is believed to be over 2,000 years old.

  • Varkala Cliff

    The striking cliffs along the beach provide panoramic views of the sea, and are lined with cafes, resorts, and shops offering a great place to relax and enjoy the scenery.

  • Kappil Lake

    A serene freshwater lake located near the sea, surrounded by lush greenery, making it a perfect spot for a quiet boat ride or a peaceful stroll.

What Food Items to Try

  • Kerala Sadya: A traditional vegetarian feast served on a banana leaf, featuring a variety of curries, rice, chutneys, and desserts like payasam.
  • Fish Curry: A flavorful and spicy curry made with fresh local fish, typically served with rice and coconut-based gravy.
  • Appam with Stew: Soft rice pancakes (appam) served with a creamy vegetable or meat stew, a typical Kerala breakfast dish.
  • Puttu and Kadala Curry: A popular breakfast dish of steamed rice cakes (puttu) served with a spicy black chickpea curry (kadala).
  • Banana Chips: Crispy and crunchy deep-fried chips made from green bananas, a popular snack in Kerala.
