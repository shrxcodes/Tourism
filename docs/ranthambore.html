About Ranthambore

Ranthambore National Park, located in Rajasthan, India, is one of the largest and most renowned national parks in the country. Known for its rich history and wildlife, Ranthambore is particularly famous for being a premier destination to spot the Royal Bengal Tiger in its natural habitat. The park covers an area of 392 square kilometers and is home to diverse ecosystems, including deciduous forests, lakes, and dry grasslands.

The park is also historically significant, with the ancient Ranthambore Fort standing within its boundaries. The fort adds to the charm of this unique wildlife destination, where history and nature intertwine seamlessly. Ranthambore is an excellent example of India's successful wildlife conservation efforts and remains one of the most visited parks in India.

Wildlife to Spot

  • Bengal Tiger

    The Bengal Tiger is the highlight of Ranthambore National Park. Visitors have the opportunity to see these magnificent predators in their natural habitat.

  • Leopard

    Leopards are also commonly spotted in Ranthambore. Their elusive nature makes sightings a rare and thrilling experience for wildlife enthusiasts.

  • Indian Wild Boar

    Wild boars roam freely in the park's grasslands and are a common sight, often seen foraging or grazing.

  • Sloth Bear

    The Sloth Bear is another rare but fascinating animal that can be spotted in Ranthambore's forested regions.

Safari Experiences

  • Jeep Safari

    The most popular safari option, a jeep safari allows visitors to explore Ranthambore's diverse landscapes and view tigers, leopards, and other wildlife in their natural habitat.

  • Canter Safari

    A larger open vehicle that can carry more people, the canter safari is an ideal way for families and groups to enjoy the park together.

  • Bird Watching Tours

    These specialized tours focus on the diverse bird species of the park, offering birdwatchers the opportunity to spot a variety of rare and migratory birds.

  • Photography Tours

    For photographers, Ranthambore offers specialized safari tours to capture the breathtaking beauty of the park's wildlife and landscapes.
