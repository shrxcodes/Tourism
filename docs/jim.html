Jim Corbett National Park

Discover the beauty and wildlife of Jim Corbett, India's first national park, located in Uttarakhand.

About Jim Corbett

Jim Corbett National Park, located in the Nainital district of Uttarakhand, India, is the oldest national park in India and the first to be established as part of the Indian wildlife conservation efforts. Founded in 1936, it is named after the famous hunter-turned-conservationist Jim Corbett, who played a pivotal role in its creation.

The park is home to diverse wildlife, including the majestic Royal Bengal Tiger, elephants, leopards, and various species of birds. Jim Corbett is known for its rich biodiversity and serene landscapes, making it a favorite destination for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts.

Wildlife to Spot

  • Royal Bengal Tiger

    The Royal Bengal Tiger is the star attraction of Jim Corbett, and spotting one during a safari is a thrilling experience.

  • Asian Elephant

    The park is home to large herds of Asian elephants, which can be seen grazing in the grasslands and forests.

  • Leopard

    Leopards, although elusive, are frequently spotted in the park's hilly and forested areas, making for an exciting wildlife experience.

  • Wild Boar

    The wild boar is a common sight in the park's grasslands and is often seen in herds, rooting for food.

  • Birds

    Jim Corbett is home to over 600 species of birds, including migratory species, waterfowl, and raptors, making it a paradise for birdwatchers.

Safari Experiences

  • Jeep Safari

    The Jeep Safari is the most popular option for tourists to explore the park, offering the best chance to spot tigers and other wildlife in their natural habitat.

  • Elephant Safari

    For a unique experience, visitors can opt for an elephant safari, which allows them to get closer to wildlife, especially tigers and elephants.

  • Canter Safari

    Ideal for larger groups, the Canter Safari allows tourists to explore the park in a large vehicle and offers a more spacious and comfortable ride.

  • Bird Watching Tours

    For bird lovers, Jim Corbett offers specialized bird watching tours to spot a wide variety of local and migratory birds.
