About Hyderabad

Hyderabad, the capital city of Telangana, is known for its rich history, beautiful architecture, and unique blend of cultures. Once ruled by the Nizams, the city is home to many ancient monuments, as well as a thriving tech industry.

Known as "The Pearl City," Hyderabad offers visitors a chance to explore ancient forts, bustling bazaars, and indulge in flavorful biryanis and street food.

Top 4 Tourist Spots in Hyderabad

  • Charminar

    Built in 1591, the Charminar is an iconic monument in the heart of the city and a symbol of Hyderabad's grandeur.

  • Golconda Fort

    An ancient fortress with a fascinating history and amazing acoustics, providing stunning views of the city.

  • Ramoji Film City

    One of the largest film studio complexes in the world, where visitors can explore movie sets and enjoy entertainment.

What Food Items to Try in Hyderabad

  • Hyderabadi Biryani: A flavorful rice dish cooked with marinated meat, aromatic spices, and saffron.
  • Haleem: A slow-cooked stew made with wheat, meat, lentils, and spices, typically served during Ramadan.
  • Mirchi Ka Salan: A spicy, tangy gravy made with green chilies and served alongside biryani.
  • Irani Chai and Osmania Biscuits: Traditional tea served with buttery, flaky biscuits from the iconic Irani cafes.
  • Qubani Ka Meetha: A sweet dish made from apricots, sugar, and a dash of cream, often served as a dessert.
