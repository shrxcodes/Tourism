About Auli

Auli is a beautiful hill station in Uttarakhand, known for its snow-covered slopes and pristine beauty. Nestled in the Garhwal Himalayas, Auli is a top skiing destination in India and offers breathtaking views of peaks like Nanda Devi and Mana Parvat.

Whether you are a skiing enthusiast or a nature lover, Auli's serene environment, coniferous forests, and adventure activities make it a perfect getaway for winter sports and scenic relaxation.

Top 4 Tourist Spots in Auli

  • Auli Ski Resort

    A popular destination for skiing, offering well-maintained slopes and ski lifts for beginners and experts alike.

  • Gurso Bugyal

    A lush green meadow that turns snowy white in winter, offering stunning panoramic views of the Himalayas.

  • Auli Ropeway

    One of the highest and longest ropeways in Asia, providing an aerial view of snow-covered landscapes.

What Food Items to Try

  • Kachmauli: Stuffed goat prepared with local spices, a delicacy in Garhwali cuisine.
  • Chainsoo: A nutritious dish made from black gram dal, rich in protein and flavor.
  • Aloo Ke Gutke: Spicy fried potatoes, a popular snack in the region.
  • Gahat Ki Dal: Horse gram lentil dish, often served with rice or roti in Uttarakhand.
  • Bal Mithai: A local sweet made from roasted khoya and coated with sugar balls.
