Bengaluru - The Garden City

Experience the dynamic blend of greenery, technology, and heritage in India’s Silicon Valley.

About Bengaluru

Bengaluru, the capital of Karnataka, is India’s leading tech hub and one of the fastest-growing cities in Asia. Also known as the Garden City, it boasts a pleasant climate, lush parks, and a lively cosmopolitan culture.

Alongside its booming IT industry, the city is also known for its palaces, temples, and vibrant nightlife, making it a unique mix of tradition and innovation.

Top 4 Tourist Spots in Bengaluru

  • Lalbagh Botanical Garden

    Spread over 240 acres, Lalbagh is home to rare plants, a glasshouse modeled after London’s Crystal Palace, and beautiful walking paths.

  • Bangalore Palace

    Inspired by England’s Windsor Castle, this grand palace features Tudor-style architecture and lavish interiors.

  • Cubbon Park

    A peaceful green oasis in the heart of the city, ideal for morning walks and weekend picnics.

  • ISKCON Temple

    One of the largest ISKCON temples in the world, it combines spiritual atmosphere with modern architecture.

What Food Items to Try in Bengaluru

  • Masala Dosa: A crispy rice crepe stuffed with spicy mashed potatoes, served with chutney and sambar.
  • Idli Vada: A common breakfast combo of soft steamed rice cakes and crispy fritters.
  • Filter Coffee: Aromatic and strong South Indian filter coffee served in traditional steel tumblers.
  • Bisi Bele Bath: A hot lentil and rice dish flavored with tamarind and spices—a true Karnataka specialty.
  • Mangalore Buns: Sweet, fluffy banana puris often enjoyed with coconut chutney or sambar.
